Geography

The nation includes more than 1200 km of coastline, more than 350 islands, amazing mountain chains, and sandy deserts. It is situated in the horn of Africa by the Red Sea.

History

One of the nations having the most traces of prehistoric human habitation.

Culture

There are nine distinct ethnic groups, each with its own set of languages, foods, music, costumes, and way of life.

Climate

Because of the elevation difference within the country, you can travel through three climate zones in one hour.
